I

Chihiro Ogino

Young girl who enters the spirit world and slowly learns courage, kindness, and responsibility. Through her journey, she grows from a frightened child into someone strong enough to protect others and herself.

II

"Haku" Nigihayami Kohakunushi

Mysterious boy who is actually a river spirit. He guides Chihiro in the spirit world, showing both kindness and strength while struggling to remember his true identity.

III

No-Face

Lonely spirit who reflects the emotions and desires of those around him. He becomes dangerous when consumed by greed, but gentle and calm when shown kindness.

IV

Yubaba

Powerful witch who rules the bathhouse in the spirit world. She is strict and intimidating, but values hard work and keeps order through magic and control.

V

Kamaji

Multi-armed spirit who operates the bathhouse’s boiler room. Though he appears intimidating at first, he is one of Chihiro's most loyal allies.
